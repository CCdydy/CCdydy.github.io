We proposed a general-purpose deepfake image detector called **Time Step Generating (TSG)**. Unlike existing methods, TSG is independent of pretraining model reconstruction abilities, specific datasets, or sampling algorithms.

We further extended this deepfake detection technique to **fake video detection**, enabling localization of manipulated frames and forged regions.
