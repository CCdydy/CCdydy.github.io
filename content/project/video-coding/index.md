With the continuous development of generative models (diffusion), this project explores a **new video encoding paradigm** starting from ultra-low bit-rates, gradually resolving bottlenecks and promoting next-generation video coding technology.

Related work includes **Bi-AGMI**, a bidirectional frame interpolation model using diffusion models for video compression and transmission. (IEEE GCCE 2025 Oral)

Also includes **FRS**, a video slicing algorithm driven by motion intensity analysis. (IEVC 2026)

This research is conducted in collaboration with **NICT Japan (情報通信研究機構)**.
